# Product Marketing Context — Advance IDAN Portal

## Product
**Name:** Advance IDAN Portal (Ada Vance)
**URL:** https://advance-idan-research.vercel.app
**Type:** Internal procurement intelligence dashboard

## What It Does
Aggregates and surfaces active water-infrastructure procurement tenders from Central American government portals (Panama IDAAN, Costa Rica AyA/SICOP, Nicaragua SISCAE, El Salvador UNAC) so the Advance team can identify bid opportunities in real time.

## Client / Owner
- **Company:** Somos Advance — water utilities contractor and government project financing in Central America
- **Owner:** Miguel Bethencourt, Head of Growth at WindMar Home / M4X Digital
- **Primary users:** Advance business development / sales team (3–8 people)

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile for the Portal)
- Role: BD / sales / project managers at Advance
- Pain: Government tender data is fragmented across 4+ country portals, each with different auth, formats, and update cadence
- Goal: Never miss a water-infrastructure tender in Panama, Costa Rica, Nicaragua, or El Salvador; know deadlines and values at a glance; forward to team with one click
- Behavior: Checks daily (morning), filters by deadline urgency and contract value, saves interesting tenders, exports to CSV for internal tracking

## Jobs To Be Done
1. **Find tenders I shouldn't miss** — scan all country sources in one view, filter by deadline / value
2. **Save and share** — bookmark for team review, export CSV / Excel
3. **Know what's urgent** — surface tenders closing within 7 days
4. **Get to the source fast** — one click to the official government portal

## Positioning
> The fastest way for the Advance BD team to track every active water-infrastructure tender across Central America — without juggling 4 government portals.

## Tone
- Professional, bilingual (Spanish primary / English secondary)
- Data-forward: numbers, dates, values front and center
- Government-serious: no marketing fluff in the data layer
- CTAs should be direct: "Ver Licitación", "Guardar", "Descargar"

## Key Metrics (what success looks like)
- All tenders clickable and link to correct source
- No console errors
- Mobile usable (team checks on phones)
- Load time < 2s
- Data freshness: updated daily at 06:00–08:00 AST via automated scrapers
